Festival Programme

The Morija Arts and Cultural Festival programme includes a variety of activities that celebrate Basotho culture, music, art, and literature. Visitors will enjoy traditional dance performances, live music, art exhibitions, storytelling sessions, and many other cultural experiences throughout the three-day festival.
